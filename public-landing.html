🔧 Simple Integration

Add 3D data visualization to your website in under 2 minutes:

1️⃣ Include the Script

<script src="https://bits/sv/sphere-viewer.js"></script>

2️⃣ Add a Container

<div id="my-sphere-viewer"></div>

3️⃣ Initialize with Your Data

<script> const viewer = new FeatrixSphereViewer(); viewer.init({ data: yourDataObject, containerId: 'my-sphere-viewer' }); </script>
